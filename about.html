/Jr UX Designer

Stef Kolin is based in Chicago, IL.

She specializes in user experience research and user centered design. She is energized by smart technology with its limitless potential to upgrade day-to-day activities for people.

Her fascination for the design of smart technology applications combined with her years of experience in merchandising strategy led her to pivot into the forward-thinking field of UX design.

She is now able to leverage her strategic experience in defining user pain points into ideating, prototyping, and testing user solutions.

At this pivotal point in her career she has had the privilege to perform the role of a product designer for conceptual projects exclusively through the Northwestern UX/UI Bootcamp.
